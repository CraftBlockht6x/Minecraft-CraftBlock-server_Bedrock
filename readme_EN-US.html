Server details

Official website: craftblock.totalh.net

For detailed address information of the server, please visit our official website.

How to Join

To join our server, please follow these steps:

  1. Open the Minecraft bedrock edition.
  2. Click on the "Game" menu.
  3. Select "Server".
  4. Slide to the bottom, click "Add External Server" and enter the server address and port. Please find the specific address information in official website.
  5. Click "Join" to connect to the server.

Server Rules
